Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I use the scanner feature?
A: Tap the scan button on the main screen. Ensure your device supports scanning and has a working camera.

Q: How do I request payment via Venmo?
A: Tap the “Request Payment via Venmo” button after entering your bill details. Make sure Venmo is installed on your device.
